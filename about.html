Our Mission

Leadership Starts Here

Founded by Thomas Mercaldo of Aquinas Consulting in Milford, Connecticut, Aquinas Eagle was created to support scouts, troop leaders, and families through premium leadership resources, educational materials, and scouting products.

Built on the belief that leadership is developed through experience, discipline, and service, our mission is to equip young people with tools that help them grow both within scouting and in everyday life.

We focus on creating resources that reinforce core values such as responsibility, teamwork, and perseverance, helping scouts build confidence as they progress through their journey.

Today, Aquinas Eagle continues to expand its reach, serving troops nationwide and supporting the next generation of leaders through meaningful, practical, and high-quality scouting materials.
