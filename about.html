About Aquinas Eagle

Aquinas Eagle creates premium scouting books, ceremony products, leadership resources, and accessories trusted by troops nationwide.

Our Mission

Leadership Starts Here

Founded by Thomas Mercaldo of Aquinas Consulting in Milford, Connecticut, Aquinas Eagle was created to support scouts, troop leaders, and families through premium leadership resources, educational materials, and scouting products.

With a focus on character, discipline, and personal growth, Aquinas Eagle helps young leaders develop the skills and confidence needed both inside and outside of scouting communities nationwide.

Leadership Starts Here

Aquinas Eagle is dedicated to helping scouts, leaders, and families build confidence, leadership, and lifelong character through premium educational resources.

Our mission is to provide products that inspire growth, discipline, and service within scouting communities nationwide.
